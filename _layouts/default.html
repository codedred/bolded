A Movement Building Project

BOLDED exists to pursuit one goal: provide a fighting chance for all underprivileged.

We are inspired by the toughness that underprivileged people shown in facing the daily grind of life, and we believe their fighting spirit represents an untapped source of talent for employers.

Documentary: “Rebrand” the Underprivileged

Our film invites decision makers to witness this fighting spirit. It's our hope that decision makers too will want to invite this hidden talent pool to join and benefit their organization. Philippines is our first filming location.

We are crowdfunding for a longer series.
