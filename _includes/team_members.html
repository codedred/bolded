The Founder

Bill Yuen

Bill is a Hong Kong serial entrepreneur. 7 startups, 4 failed, 3 exit. He founded his first venture at age 18 (entertainment), second at age 22 (wearable technology), fourth at age 26 (media), and seventh at age 30 (artificial intelligence).

At age 31, Bill founded EntrepreneurHK — a private non­profit devoted to develop HK’s startup ecosystem

At age 32, Bill is shortlisted for the Talent Unleashed Inspirational Leadership Award 2014 – APAC’s largest technology awards program – for his effort of driving innovation using leadership. Virgin team Sir Richard Branson and Apple co­team Steve Wozniak headed the judging panel.

At age 33, Bill traveled to Tibet and Nepal, and climbed Mount Everest. In his trip, he has witnessed poverty first hand in rural China. He was taken with the idea that with his experience and creativity he could harness his expertise and money to better the lives of people in the developing world.

Today at age 34, Bill founded Project CODED – a project that aims to equip underprivileged young men and women job­ready programming skill and land them in a high­tech job – the type of jobs can effectively end poverty in their lives. Philippines is the launch region.

In the same year, he produced a short documentary series and launched a “movement building” campaign named BOLDED. The goal is to change the world’s perception about underprivileged young people.

Ruel Antipuesto

Cinematographer

Ruel Dahis Antipuesto is an engineer turned filmmaker. When the digital filmmaking revolution happened in the late 90's, he taught himself audiovisual storytelling by making animations & documentaries. In 2004, the animated short film, "The Snake's Pit" won him his first national award. In 2007, the full length movie he co­directed won in India & was in exhibition in Germany, Korea and the US. Soon after, his documentary projects have been acquired for distribution & one won the grand prize in the U.N. MDG competition. Through the years he has accumulated several awards as his works have been included in various local and international competitions and exhibitions, including Portugal, UK, Germany, Korea, USA, India, France, Spain, Venice, Canada, Switzerland and Asean region. Lately, Ruel is getting more involved in international projects while running a marketing communications company in Cebu and continue making movies at the same time. As a hobby, he does audiovisual reverse engineering over a glass of iced soda.

Thanks for saying yes to all the 5am call times (blame Sharon). And your jokes, your black Under Armor t­shirt, and of course your awesome shots.
