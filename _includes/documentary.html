Documentary: “Rebranding” Underprivileged People

We are filming a short documentary series that aim to give underprivileged people a new and more fitted image

— a “fighter” image.

The fact that they need to go through walls everyday to survive is a testament of their toughness — an attribute to win in the business world. We believe the new image can lead to better employment opportunities for the lower­income people. Philippines is our first filming location.
