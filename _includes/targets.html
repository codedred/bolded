Your own BOLDED event

We’ve developed an “action kit” for enthusiastic individuals who are motivated to organize their own BOLDED events. Event formats are flexible: private screening, private dinner, speaking event, discussion forums, etc.

The “action kit” contains our documentary videos, dedicated website for their events, event RSVP tools, dedicated online fundraising page, and social media support to help promote their events.

Attendees are encouraged to discuss the documentary and come up with unique Call for Action ideas.
